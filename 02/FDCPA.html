What is FDCPA?

The Fair Debt-Collections Practices Act is a federal statute of the US.

It guards everyday citizens from unethical practices and behaviors.

Common Forms of FDCPA Harassment

  • Repeated or Excessive Phone Calls
  • Calls to the Workplace After Being Asked to Stop
  • Contacting Third Parties
  • Use of Threatening or Abusive Language
  • False or Misleading Statements
  • Threats of Arrest or Imprisonment
  • Failing to Identify Themselves Properly
  • Trying to Collect Debts Not Owed
  • Failing to Validate the Debt
  • Calling After a Cease and Desist Request
