G-code Basics

G-code is the language used to control CNC machines. Here are some basics:

  • G0 / G1: Move command (rapid or controlled move).
  • X and Y: Specify coordinates. (Coordinates are interpreted as offsets from the center.)

Examples:

  • G1 X50 Y100 – Moves to a point 50 units right and 100 units up (relative to the center).
  • G0 X-50 Y-25 – Rapid move to 50 units left and 25 units down.
